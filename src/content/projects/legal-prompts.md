## Overview

A systematic prompt engineering library developed for the ALQAC 2023 Legal Question Answering competition (Task 2), resulting in **1st Prize** at IEEE KSE 2023.

## Approach

- Designed chain-of-thought and few-shot prompt templates tailored to Vietnamese legal document structure.
- Evaluated multiple LLMs on a Vietnamese legal QA benchmark, tracking accuracy per question type.
- Ablation study across prompt formats: zero-shot, few-shot, retrieval-augmented, and CoT variants.

## Results

- **1st Prize ALQAC 2023** — Legal Question Answering Task 2.
- Published as IEEE KSE 2023 paper: [ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10299426](https://ieeexplore.ieee.org/document/10299426).
