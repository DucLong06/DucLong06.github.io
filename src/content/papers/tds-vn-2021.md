## Overview

**Toward Data Science Vietnam (TDS VN)** is an annual data science competition for Vietnamese students and early-career engineers, covering machine learning, data analytics, and applied mathematics.

## Competition

- **Track**: Data Analytics and Math for Beginners
- **Result**: 3rd Prize nationally
- **Year**: 2021

## Topics Covered

The submission demonstrated applied data analysis skills including exploratory data analysis (EDA), statistical modelling, and data visualisation on a Vietnamese real-world dataset.

This early competition result, achieved while still at Electric Power University, marked the beginning of a focused trajectory in AI and data engineering — leading to the Sao Khuê Award (2022) and IEEE KSE paper (2023).
