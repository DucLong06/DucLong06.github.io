## Abstract

This paper presents a system for automated Vietnamese legal question answering leveraging large language models with structured prompt engineering. Developed during an exchange research period at the Japan Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (JAIST), Nguyen Lab, the system achieved first place in Task 2 of the Automated Legal Question Answering Competition (ALQAC) 2023.

## Key Contributions

- Systematic evaluation of prompt strategies (zero-shot, few-shot, chain-of-thought) on Vietnamese legal corpora.
- Retrieval-augmented generation pipeline combining BM25 retrieval with LLM answer synthesis.
- Publicly released prompt templates in the [Legal-Prompts](https://github.com/DucLong06/Legal-Prompts) repository (★11).
