## Overview

The **Sao Khuê Award** is Vietnam's most prestigious technology prize, awarded annually by VINASA to outstanding software products and IT services.

AX-OCR received the Sao Khuê Award 2022 as Vietnam's leading Vietnamese-language OCR engine, developed by the core team at Cyber Eye Technology.

## Technical Highlights

- **98% accuracy** on digit recognition; **96%** on handwritten Vietnamese characters, names, and addresses; **97%** on handwritten dates.
- Offline C++ CPU-only inference — no GPU required, deployable on edge scanners and printers.
- PDF→DOCX conversion with ~80% formatting fidelity on non-searchable scanned documents.
- Handles ID cards, insurance forms, resumes, and diverse Vietnamese document types.
